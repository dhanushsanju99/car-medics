Our Story

Started in one bay.
Trusted across Kannur.

Car Medics began as a single-lift garage in Pilathara, with a simple idea — that fitting accessories is a craft, not a transaction. Nearly a decade later, that's still how every car leaves us: considered, not just completed.

We hold genuine and premium aftermarket stock, and every install happens in-house, by technicians who treat your car the way they'd treat their own.

What We Do
